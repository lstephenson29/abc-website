Company History

Key Milestones

2009
Dr. Edward O. Shaffer II founds Advanced Battery Concepts in Clare, Michigan with a vision to commercialize bipolar lead battery technology for large-format energy storage applications.
2012
First GreenSeal® prototype validated, proving that bipolar lead architecture could achieve the energy density, cycle life, and temperature performance required for critical infrastructure deployment.
2015
Key patents granted for bipolar architecture, securing foundational intellectual property protection for ABC's cell design, sealing technology, and manufacturing processes.
2018
Manufacturing 4.0 facility established. Automated production lines for GreenSeal® batteries at the Clare, Michigan headquarters, enabling consistent quality at commercial scale.
2020
EverGreenSeal™ platform launched. Next-generation bipolar lead technology engineered specifically for utility-scale and long-duration energy storage applications.
2023
6 global manufacturing licensees signed, extending ABC's bipolar lead technology to manufacturing partners across multiple continents and markets.
2025
Commercial-scale Battery Barn program initiated. Modular, phased-deployment energy storage systems for municipal utilities and grid infrastructure operators.

Manufacturing

Built in Michigan. Sourced Domestically.

ABC's manufacturing headquarters in Clare, Michigan houses automated GreenSeal® production lines with domestically sourced materials. Zero Foreign Entity of Concern supply chain risk. Full ITC domestic content eligibility.

Clare, Michigan Facility

Our Manufacturing 4.0 facility in Clare, Michigan is purpose-built for bipolar lead battery production. Automated stacking, sealing, and filling processes ensure consistent quality across every module that leaves the line.

Domestic sourcing of all critical materials eliminates the supply chain vulnerabilities and FEOC concerns that plague lithium-ion manufacturers dependent on overseas mineral processing.

  • Automated GreenSeal® production lines
  • Domestically sourced lead and materials
  • Zero FEOC supply chain risk
