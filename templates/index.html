Scalable Deployment

One Modular Unit. Three Ways to Deploy.

The CEES 48 kWh cartridge is ABC's core building block. Depending on project scale, it deploys in outdoor-rated enclosures for small installations, BoxBe ISO containers for mid-range projects, or constructed Battery Barn facilities for utility-scale storage.

01 / Outdoor Enclosures

Direct Deployment

Outdoor-rated NEMA3R/IP55 enclosures linked to wall-mounted inverters. Ideal for remote sites, microgrids, and island infrastructure. As deployed at Manitou Islands National Park.

02 / BoxBe Containers

Containerized Storage

10-foot ISO containers (384 kWh) or 20-foot ISO containers (768 kWh). Pre-integrated, pre-tested, transportable by standard flatbed. Deploy in weeks, not months.

03 / Battery Barn

Utility-Scale Facility

Purpose-built warehouse housing 432 CEES cartridges. 5 MW / 20 MWh in 6,000 square feet. No HVAC. No fire suppression. Where lithium needs 3 to 5 acres, ABC fits in a single building.
