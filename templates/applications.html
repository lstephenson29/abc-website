Deployment Methodology

One Unit. Scale to Any Project.

The CEES 48 kWh cartridge is ABC's modular building block. Depending on scale, it deploys in three configurations: outdoor-rated enclosures for distributed installations, BoxBe ISO containers for mid-range projects, or constructed Battery Barn facilities for utility-scale storage.

Outdoor Enclosures

Direct Deployment for Distributed Sites

CEES units deploy in outdoor-rated NEMA3R/IP55 enclosures, linked to wall-mounted inverters. No building required. Ideal for remote sites, microgrids, island infrastructure, and installations where space is limited or construction is impractical. As deployed at the Manitou Islands project for the National Park Service.

NEMA3R/IP55 Wall-Mounted Inverters Remote Deployable No Building Required

BoxBe Containers

Containerized Storage, Ready to Ship

Pre-integrated CEES cartridges housed in standard 10-foot ISO containers (384 kWh) or 20-foot ISO containers (768 kWh). Factory tested, transportable by standard flatbed, and deployable in weeks. Outdoor rated with IP55/NEMA3R protection and greater than 90% round-trip efficiency.

384 kWh (10ft) 768 kWh (20ft) IP55/NEMA3R Pre-Integrated

Battery Barn

Utility-Scale in 6,000 Square Feet

Purpose-built warehouse facility housing 432 CEES cartridges for 5 MW / 20 MWh capacity. No HVAC, no fire suppression, no augmentation. Where lithium-ion demands 3 to 5 acres for equivalent capacity, ABC fits in a single building. 20-year warranty and 15-year daily cycle life warranty with zero augmentation cost.

5 MW / 20 MWh 6,000 sq ft No HVAC 20-Year Warranty

Proven Deployment

Manitou Islands, National Park Service

72 kW / 288 kWh Off-Grid System

Deployed for the United States Federal Government at Sleeping Bear Dunes National Shoreline. CEES outdoor enclosures with wall-mounted inverters powering all island infrastructure. Balanced three-phase 208V system configuration with two inverters per phase. Continuous power delivery in a remote, environmentally sensitive location.

  • National Park Service, Sleeping Bear Dunes National Shoreline
  • 72 kW / 288 kWh off-grid configuration
  • Balanced three-phase 208V output
  • Two inverters per phase, six total
  • Remote island deployment, no grid connection
