System Architecture

Cartridge Matrix Configuration

432 CEES cartridges arranged in a 4-high by 6-row by 18-column matrix. Each cartridge contains 48 GC07481M bipolar lead modules delivering 50.4 kWh at 288V nominal. Cartridges are mechanically fastened to adjacent units with a maximum 4-high stack limit per structural analysis.

  • DC System Voltage: 1,152V (4 cartridges in series)
  • 108 parallel strings for current distribution
  • 10-foot row spacing for forklift maintenance access
  • High-voltage DC electrical room physically separated from battery area

Safety and Compliance

No HVAC. No Fire Suppression. No Compromise.

01

Non-Flammable Chemistry

Aqueous sulfuric acid electrolyte absorbed in AGM separators. No organic solvent. No flammable liquid. Zero fire propagation risk. Eliminates NFPA 855 thermal barrier and explosion venting requirements.

02

Zero Thermal Runaway

Lead-acid electrochemistry does not support exothermic thermal runaway. The battery cannot self-heat to ignition. Eliminates the catastrophic failure mode that drives prescriptive requirements for lithium-ion installations.

03

Standard Construction

Deploys in standard commercial warehouse structures. No explosion-rated construction, no fire-rated separation walls, no active suppression systems. Reduces construction cost and accelerates permitting timelines.

04

Hydrogen Management

Sealed VRLA design with flame arrestors on every vent port. Continuous mechanical ventilation maintains hydrogen below 1.0% by volume, well below the 4.0% lower flammability limit.
